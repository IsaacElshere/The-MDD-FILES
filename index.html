H.I.N.D.E.R. Protocol

The H.I.N.D.E.R. principle is the MDD's core survival framework for civilians and employees who encounter potential Mimic activity. Memorize it. It may save your life.

H
Hide

Hiding is important when it comes to survival. When hiding, make sure to hide in a place with lights that are on. If the lights aren't on "it" will find you.

I
Identify

Make sure you know your enemy. Identify the type of threat you are dealing with. Check the instructional tape to see your options. After you have identified the threat, contact your nearest police station.

N
Neutralize

Try to execute the mimic. They are vulnerable to the light. ALWAYS have the lights on. Mimics are weak in specific situations, examples: In light, in tight spaces etc.

D
Detect

Make sure to watch all entrances to your home, whether that be with cameras, or with your own eyes. Take a walk through your home every night before bed. Look thoroughly, mimics are extremely hard to spot in the dark, which is how most catch their victims.

E
Emphasize

While calling the police, make sure to emphasize any abnormal features of the threat. The police know what each abnormality means, and what kind of entity it is connected to.

R
Record

While hiding, make sure to document the threat's behavior. Write it down, or record it on your cellular device. Once IT leaves, or IF IT leaves, report the encounter to the M.D.D.
